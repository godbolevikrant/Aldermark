Twelve years of saying no to most engagements.

Our Story

Started as a two-person diligence shop. Stayed small on purpose.

Aldermark opened in 2014 with a single client and a simple rule: take fewer mandates than you could, and stay on each one until the numbers actually move.

That rule still governs how we grow. We cap active engagements per partner, which means every client gets a senior person in the room — not a deck built by someone three levels removed from the work. It also means we turn down work that doesn't fit, more often than we take it on.

What We Believe

Four working principles, not a mission statement.

I

Say no often

We take roughly one in four enquiries. Capacity is the product.

II

Senior, not scaled

Every engagement has a named partner who stays for its full length.

III

Numbers before opinions

No recommendation is drafted before the diagnostic is finished.

IV

Stay until it moves

We measure ourselves on the metric the client cared about at the start.
