Home / Services

What we do, and why it's structured this way.

01

Growth Architecture

We build the three-year plan for revenue, headcount, and product sequencing — then break it into the next two quarters your team can actually execute. Includes market-sizing, channel prioritization, and a working operating model.

02

Capital Structuring

Raise preparation, cap-table cleanup, and recapitalization strategy. We sit on your side of the table through term-sheet negotiation, not just the data room.

03

Operating Review

A 6-week diagnostic across unit economics, org design, and process — delivered as a ranked list of the changes most likely to move margin this year.

04

Market Entry

Go-to-market design for a new geography or segment, including regulatory mapping, pricing localization, and a partner shortlist with first-call notes.

05

M&A Diligence

Commercial and operational diligence on the buy or sell side — a clear-eyed read on synergy claims before they go in front of your board.

06

Board Advisory

A standing seat or observer role for founders who want an experienced second read on governance, succession, and major capital decisions.
